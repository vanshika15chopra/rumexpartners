About Us

Rumex Ventures is headed by Neeraj Gupta. It invests proprietary capital across both listed and unlisted spaces.In the listed space, we focus on fundamentally strong companies with scalable potential and sound governance. In the unlisted space, we back emerging businesses — particularly in the SME and mid-market segments — where capital, structure, and strategy can create exponential value.

Our investment philosophy

We blends discipline, insight, and structure. We invest in high-potential companies led by visionary founders, providing strategic capital, operational partnership, and board-level guidance. Our approach combines rigorous market analysis, scalability assessment, and risk management to drive sustainable growth and position businesses for long-term market leadership and potential public listing.
